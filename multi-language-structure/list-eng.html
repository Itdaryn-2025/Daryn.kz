Tursunov Gazhdembek Kuanishevich

Director of the Republican Scientific and Practical Center "Daryn"

By the order of the Minister of Education of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Gazhdembek Kuanishevich Tursunov was appointed Director of the Republican Scientific and Practical Center "Daryn".

Gazhdembek Tursunov was born in 1975 in Pavlodar region.

He graduated from the Pavlodar Pedagogical College named after V. Vorovsky, Kainar University, and the Innovative Eurasian University.

Master of Economics and Business.

He began his career as a teacher of Kazakh language and literature at the Oktyabrskaya Secondary School in the Irtysh district of Pavlodar region.

From 1993 to 2011, he worked as a teacher and deputy director in secondary education organizations in the Irtysh district of Pavlodar region.

From 2011 to 2013, he worked as the chief specialist of the Department of Education of Pavlodar region.

From 2013 to 2019, he headed the regional educational and methodological center for additional education "Ertis Daryny" in Pavlodar region.

From 2019 to 2020, he worked as Deputy Director of RSE "RSPC "Daryn".

From 2020 to 2023, he held the position of Director of the Center for the Development of Education and Psychological Services "Astana Daryny".
